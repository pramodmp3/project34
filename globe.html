Stackly: A World of Production Excellence

Our footprint spans continents, ensuring global access to quality pharmaceuticals.

Spanning Strategic Continents

Stackly maintains a **robust and strategically located global network** of development and manufacturing facilities. This widespread presence allows us to serve diverse regulatory markets efficiently, manage regional supply chain risks, and provide customized logistics solutions, including specialized **cold chain management**.

We are committed to increasing access to vital medicines by placing our capabilities where they matter most. Our facilities are unified by a single, stringent **Global Quality Management System** (QMS), guaranteeing consistency whether a product is manufactured in North America, Europe, or Asia.

Integrated Global Capabilities

High-Volume Sterile Fill/Finish

Dedicated facilities in Europe and Asia focusing on **high-speed aseptic filling** for injectables and vaccines, supported by robotics and isolator technology.

Potent Compound Handling

Specialized containment suites in the US and Germany designed for the safe handling and manufacture of **Highly Potent Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (HPAPIs)**.

Scalable Solid Dosage Production

Mass production capabilities for tablets and capsules in our Indian and North American hubs, utilizing continuous manufacturing technology for **efficiency and speed**.

Verified Cold Chain Integrity

End-to-end temperature-controlled transport verified by real-time IoT monitoring across all transit points.

Multi-Modal Distribution Hubs

Strategic placement near major air and sea freight lanes for rapid and reliable delivery to over 80 countries.

Blockchain Security & Traceability

Utilizing decentralized ledger technology to ensure tamper-proof traceability and authentication of every shipment.
