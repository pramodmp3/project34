API Production at Stackly

Producing High-Purity Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients with Unrivaled Quality and Scale.

The Foundation of Medicine

**Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs)** are the biologically active components in a finished drug product. Their quality is non-negotiable, directly influencing the efficacy and safety of the final medicine.

Stackly specializes in cGMP manufacturing of complex small-molecule APIs and advanced intermediates. Our vertical integration and closed-system chemistry ensure complete control over purity, chirality, and consistency, meeting the most stringent global regulatory demands.

Testing Protocols

Our analytical team uses state-of-the-art instruments for method validation and routine testing, including HPLC, GC, Mass Spec, and NMR. Every batch is released only after passing stringent tests for heavy metals, residual solvents, and polymorphic form.

We provide complete documentation for regulatory filings (DMF, ASMF) to accelerate your time-to-market.

Advanced Facilities & Tech

Our purpose-built API manufacturing facility features flexible modular cleanrooms and high-containment suites suitable for handling highly potent compounds. We rely on advanced **distributed control systems (DCS)** for real-time batch monitoring and consistent process parameters.
