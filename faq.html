Frequently Asked Questions

Answers concerning our research, compliance, and commitment to innovation in the biotech sector.

Our core focus is on **novel small molecule compounds** and **advanced gene therapy vectors**. We utilize proprietary AI platforms to accelerate target identification and synthesis, prioritizing precision medicine outcomes.
Safety and efficacy are ensured through rigorous **Phase 0 to Phase III clinical trials**, validated by independent CROs, and adhering strictly to ICH-GCP guidelines. Preclinical testing involves extensive in-vitro and in-vivo modeling.
Yes. Stackly maintains continuous compliance with the **FDA (U.S.)**, **EMA (Europe)**, and all regional regulatory authorities. Our Quality Assurance team enforces strict GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice) standards.
AI is fundamental to our process, used for **predictive toxicology**, high-throughput virtual screening, and optimizing synthesis pathways, significantly reducing the time required to advance candidates to preclinical stages.
Eligibility depends on the specific therapeutic area and trial phase. Please visit the **Clinical Trials** section of our official website or consult with your healthcare provider for current enrollment information.
We operate fully automated, state-of-the-art facilities that meet **cGMP (current Good Manufacturing Practice)** standards, focusing on continuous flow chemistry for purity and scalability.
Sustainability is a priority. We employ **green chemistry principles**, reduce waste through optimized synthesis routes, and minimize the environmental footprint of our research labs and facilities.
All proprietary research data is protected by **end-to-end encryption**, multi-factor authentication, and secure, isolated cloud environments compliant with global data protection regulations (e.g., GDPR, HIPAA).
Our primary therapeutic pipelines target **neurodegenerative diseases**, oncology (specifically novel immunotherapies), and rare genetic disorders.
Drug development timelines vary, but typically range from **10 to 15 years** from initial discovery to regulatory approval, including extensive preclinical and clinical phases.
Yes, collaboration with leading **academic research centers** is essential. We actively seek partnerships through our R&D outreach program to explore new mechanistic pathways.
Investigators are selected based on their **expertise, track record** in the relevant therapeutic area, ethical conduct, and access to the required patient populations and facilities.
The BLA is an application submitted to the FDA for permission to introduce or deliver a **biological product** (like vaccines or therapeutic proteins) into interstate commerce.
Our primary corporate and R&D headquarters are situated in the **Boston/Cambridge biotechnology cluster**, with satellite facilities in major global research hubs.
For all media inquiries, please direct your requests to **media@stacklypharma.com**. Please allow 24-48 hours for a response from our communications department.
